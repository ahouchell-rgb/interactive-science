Where this gets you

This is genuinely undergraduate physics. The arrows are illustrative — they show direction and approximate strength, not Maxwell-precise vectors.

What's true: fields exist throughout space, the wire is full of electrons before you start, and energy travels through the field surrounding the wire — not through the wire itself.
